The NuWho Doctors!

A northern accent. Big smile, and an offbeat sense of humour… The Ninth Doctor was the bruised but brilliant ‘last of the Time Lords’.

The Tenth Doctor was a charismatic mixture of apparent opposites… Kind and sensitive, only countered by his inflated ego at times.

The Eleventh Doctor was an excited explorer of the universe, with a keen intelligence that means he often notices what everyone else has missed.

Initially stern and ruthless, but in reality kind and forgiving, the Twelfth Doctor was the first incarnation after the end of The Siege of Trenzalore.

The Thirteenth Doctor was a charismatic and confident explorer, championing fairness and kindness wherever she can.

After The Master's attempt to steal The Doctor's body, the need to regenerate came... and The Edge Doctor was born.

The Fifteenth Doctor is a future regeneration; his personality and motives are still yet unknown...
